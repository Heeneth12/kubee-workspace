How Stock Adjustments Work

Stock Impact Adjustments instantly add or remove stock based on the reason provided. This ensures your physical counts match your digital records.
Batch Allocation Deductions are pulled directly from the specified batch number. If "Auto" is used, the system processes it using standard FIFO logic.
Permanent Record This action creates an irreversible ledger entry. All adjustments are logged with a timestamp and user ID for audit compliance.
